🏭 How the Factory works

Your video ideas are ore. You build a little assembly line that carries each idea through the stations — and you are the filter at the two steps that need a human: the Sorter and Packaging.

Build the line (left toolbar)

  1. ⛏ Drill — drop it on a glowing ore tile. It mines one idea at a time. Press R to aim its arrow at your belt.
  2. ⇉ Belt — click-drag to lay a conveyor from the drill onward. Belts plug straight into machines and the hub.
  3. Stations — drop them in order: 🔀 Sorter → ✍️ Content Doc → 📦 Packaging → 🎙️ → ✂️ → 🚀, then a 🏠 Hub at the end. Each machine is 2×2 tiles.

Where you come in (🫵)

Two stations stop and wait for you — they pulse gold and count up in 🫵 Your call at the top:

  • 🔀 Sorter — two ideas face off. Click the machine and pick the one you'd actually make (or “neither”). Your morning filter. Passed ideas go to 🗑 Discarded — recoverable.
  • 📦 Packaging — click it to choose your winning titles, then the thumbnail.

The other stations run on their own. Click any machine any time to see what it's doing and what it produced.

Make it yours (sandbox)

Hit ✎ Stations in the toolbar to build your own pipeline — hide stations you don't use, or add your own boxes (e.g. split Packaging into a Thumbnail step and a Title step). Custom stations are pass-throughs, so you can experiment with the flow freely.

Good to know

Finished assets land in the 📦 Warehouse — hit the › in its header to hide it. The ↦ Grabber is optional (bridging a gap / side-feeding). Hold ⌘ and scroll (or pinch the trackpad) to zoom — or use the ➖ / ➕ buttons.

Select, copy & bulk-erase

Shift-drag across the floor to select a region (machines, belts, the lot). Then ⌘C to copy and ⌘V to drop a movable copy — click to stamp it, Esc to stop. Delete wipes the selection. To erase a bunch fast, hold right-click and drag over everything you want gone.

Keys: 1–9 pick a tool · R rotate · shift-drag select · ⌘C/⌘V copy/paste · Delete erase selection · right-drag bulk-erase · space pause. Sorter & Content Doc call real AI; Voiceover / Edit / Publish are honest stubs.
